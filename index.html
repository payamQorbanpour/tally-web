Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Tally?
No. Tally works fully offline using local storage on your phone. Sign in only when you want your data to follow you across devices.
Is there a recurring subscription?
Never. Tally uses one-time Passes — buy a Night Out, Trip, or Explorer Pass when you actually need premium tools. We never auto-renew.
What's in the free tier?
Unlimited groups, unlimited expenses, equal splits, live balances, and the settle-up view. AI receipt scanning, advanced splits, and cloud sync are unlocked by a Pass.
Which currencies and languages are supported?
Track expenses in any major currency. The app interface is available in English, Spanish, and Persian — with full right-to-left layout support.
What happens to my data when a Pass expires?
Everything stays. Your groups, expenses, balances, and old splits remain editable. Only premium tools — AI scanning, advanced splits on new expenses — pause until you extend.
